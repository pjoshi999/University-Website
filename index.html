Université de Gandhinagar

Université de Gandhinagar is a public technical university located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India. It has been declared to be an Institute of National Importance
by the Government of India. Established in 2008, Université de Gandhinagar campus is spread over 400 acres of land along the river Sabarmati.
